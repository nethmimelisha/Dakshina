About us

Dakshina Cooperative Savings Bank

We're Dedicated to Supporting Small Businesses

Welcome to Dakshina Cooperative Savings Bank in Sri Lanka, where our commitment is to elevate the quality of life for low-income Sri Lankan families through a spectrum of microfinance initiatives. Our clientele encompasses artisans, students, fishermen, vendors, laborers, and business owners, embodying our essence as a bank for the people's development.

As you explore the links below, you'll discover more about our institution and our unwavering dedication to providing high-quality, innovative, and competitive financial products and services. Our aim is to ensure the highest possible return in the market on shareholders' capital.

We offer specialized products and services tailored to our valued customers, including Dakshina Cooperative Savings Bank itself, the cooperative sector, and various community-based organizations and non-governmental organizations. To accomplish this, we're expanding our outreach through our existing network and harnessing modern banking technology.

What truly sets us apart is our commitment to nurturing a highly skilled, motivated team of employees. Their dedication to service excellence drives our primary objectives: ensuring the financial stability of the bank and contributing to national development.
