Elevating Finance:
Quality, Innovation, and Edge.

Dakshina Cooperative Savings Bank is a distinguished financial institution dedicated to enhancing the dynamics of Sri Lanka's financial landscape, particularly for small enterprises at every juncture of their growth journey – from inception and expansion to maintaining a competitive edge.

We are steadfast in our mission to continually broaden the horizons of financial opportunities and providers available to businesses, injecting greater vitality and diversity into the market. Our carefully crafted programs are meticulously designed to cater to a spectrum of enterprises, whether they are start-ups with soaring ambitions, high-growth ventures poised for success, or simply viable but undercapitalized businesses.

Moreover, within our financial ecosystem, we proudly house Dakshina Cooperative Savings Bank, our business arm. This entity actively invests in lenders supporting Sri Lankan companies, fostering a robust environment for entrepreneurship and financial prosperity.
